Chocolate Chip Banana Bread 2-3 ripe bananas, mashed 1/3 cup melted unsalted butter 1 tsp baking soda Pinch of salt 3/4 cup granulated sugar 1 large egg, beaten 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 cup mini chocolate chips 10 min 1 hour Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan. In a large bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas. Mix in the baking soda and salt, then stir in the sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Gradually add the flour, mixing until just combined. Gently fold in the mini chocolate chips. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely. 10 slices

Cinnamon Swirl Bread 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 tsp baking powder ½ tsp baking soda 1 tsp ground cinnamon ½ tsp salt ¾ cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs 1 cup sour cream ½ cup unsalted butter, softened 1 tsp vanilla extract ¼ cup brown sugar, packed 1 tbsp ground cinnamon (for swirl) 15 min 1 hour Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon for the swirl. Pour half of the batter into the loaf pan, then sprinkle half of the cinnamon-sugar mixture over it. Add the remaining batter and swirl with a knife to mix the cinnamon filling in the center. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the loaf cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. 10 slices

Lemon Blueberry Loafs 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 tsp baking powder ½ tsp baking soda ¼ tsp salt 1 cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs ½ cup unsalted butter, softened 1 tsp vanilla extract 2 tbsp lemon zest ½ cup fresh lemon juice 1 cup fresh blueberries (or frozen, unthawed) ½ cup sour cream 15 min 1 hour Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, beat the sugar and butter together until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined. Gently fold in the blueberries and sour cream. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and spread it evenly. Bake for 50-55 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the loaf cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. 10 slices
