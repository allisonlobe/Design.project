Cupcakes


Recipe Name Ingredients Prep Time Cook Time Servings Steps
Vanilla Cupcakes 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour 1 ½ tsp baking powder ½ tsp salt ¾ cup unsalted butter, softened 1 cup granulated sugar 2 large eggs 1 tbsp vanilla extract ½ cup whole milk 15 min 20 min Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, about 3-4 minutes. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Gradually add the dry ingredients in two parts, alternating with the milk, starting and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting. 12

Chocolate Cupcakes 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 ½ cups granulated sugar ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder 1 tsp baking powder 1 tsp baking soda ½ tsp salt 2 large eggs 1 cup whole milk ½ cup vegetable oil 2 tsp vanilla extract 1 cup boiling water 15 min 20 min Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract, and beat until smooth. Gradually stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin, but that’s okay). Divide the batter evenly into the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cupcakes cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting. 12

Red Velvet Cupcakes 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 cup granulated sugar 1 tbsp cocoa powder 1 ½ tsp baking powder ½ tsp baking soda ½ tsp salt 1 cup vegetable oil 2 large eggs 1 tsp vanilla extract 1 tbsp red food coloring ½ cup buttermilk 1 tsp white vinegar 15 min 20 min Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl, beat the oil and eggs together until well combined. Add the vanilla extract and red food coloring, and mix until the batter is uniform in color. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the buttermilk, starting and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Stir in the vinegar until fully incorporated. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cupcakes cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting. 12
